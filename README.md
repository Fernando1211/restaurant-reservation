## Yamato - Restaurant Reservation System

Yamato is a modern and intuitive table reservation system designed to enhance the customer experience and streamline restaurant reservation management. With a user-friendly interface and efficient features, Yamato allows users to book their tables quickly and easily, while restaurants maintain full control over their availability and customer flow.

Whether for small bistros or large restaurant chains, Yamato offers a flexible and scalable solution, ensuring better organization, reduced waiting times, and a more enjoyable dining experience for everyone.
